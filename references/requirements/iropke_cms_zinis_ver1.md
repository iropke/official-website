# ZINIS™ Webzine CMS Page Definition

---

## 1. Page Role

The ZINIS™ page introduces a **dedicated webzine CMS for enterprises and public organizations**.

It must:
- Replace outdated offline magazines and inefficient webzine systems
- Emphasize **ready-to-use publishing infrastructure**
- Highlight **AI and search visibility readiness**
- Position ZINIS™ as a **complete publishing system, not just a CMS**

---

## 2. Core Positioning

### One-line Definition
ZINIS™ is a full-package webzine CMS designed for organizations to publish, manage, and distribute content optimized for both users and AI-driven discovery.

### Core Concept
From Publishing → To Discoverability

### Strategic Direction
- Not a generic CMS
- Not a blog tool
- A **structured editorial platform**
