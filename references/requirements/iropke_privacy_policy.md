# Privacy Policy Page Definition (EU & North America Focus)

---

## 1. Page Role

This page provides a **clear, structured, and legally aligned privacy policy**  
for users in Europe (GDPR) and North America (CCPA/CPRA).

It must:
- Ensure transparency
- Provide easy navigation
- Support compliance
- Build trust

---

## 2. Layout Structure

```plaintext
[Left Sidebar - Table of Contents]
[Right Content Area - Policy Details]
```

### Behavior
- Clicking a menu item scrolls to the corresponding section (anchor)
- Active section is highlighted in sidebar
- Sticky sidebar on scroll

## 4. Content Sections (Right Area)

## 4.1 Introduction
**Anchor ID: #introduction**

We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and manage information.

---

## 4.2 Data We Collect
**Anchor ID: #data-collection**

We may collect:
- Personal identification data (name, email)
- Usage data (pages visited, interactions)
- Device and technical data (IP, browser type)

---

## 4.3 How We Use Data
**Anchor ID: #data-usage**

We use data to:
- Provide and improve services
- Communicate with users
- Ensure security
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 4.4 Legal Basis (GDPR)
**Anchor ID: #legal-basis**

Under GDPR, we process data based on:
- Consent
- Contractual necessity
- Legal obligations
- Legitimate interests

---

## 4.5 Data Sharing
**Anchor ID: #data-sharing**

We may share data with:
- Service providers
- Legal authorities when required
- Partners under strict agreements

---

## 4.6 International Transfers
**Anchor ID: #international-transfers**

Data may be transferred outside the EU.  
We ensure safeguards such as:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC)
- Adequacy decisions

---

## 4.7 Data Retention
**Anchor ID: #data-retention**

We retain data only as long as necessary for:
- Service provision
- Legal compliance
- Business purposes

---

## 4.8 Your Rights (EU)
**Anchor ID: #eu-rights**

Under GDPR, users have rights to:
- Access data
- Correct data
- Delete data
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent

---

## 4.9 Your Rights (California)
**Anchor ID: #ca-rights**

Under CCPA/CPRA:
- Right to know
- Right to delete
- Right to opt-out of sale
- Right to non-discrimination

---

## 4.10 Cookies & Tracking
**Anchor ID: #cookies**

We use cookies for:
- Analytics
- Performance
- Personalization

Users can manage preferences via browser settings.

---

## 4.11 Security Measures
**Anchor ID: #security**

- Monitoring systems

## 4.12 Children’s Privacy
**Anchor ID: #children**

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 4.13 Updates to Policy
