# iropke NIX™ 페이지 정의 (기획 정리본)

---

## 1. Page Role

NIX™ page is not just a service introduction.  
It must guide the user through a clear psychological flow:

1. Problem Awareness  
→ “We already have this issue.”

2. Perspective Shift  
→ “This is not just a legal issue.”

3. Solution Expectation  
→ “This can be structured and solved.”

4. Trust Building  
→ “They understand real operations.”

5. Action  
→ “Let’s inquire.”

---

## 2. Core Positioning

### One-line Definition
NIX™ is an AI-powered digital risk review service that analyzes and structures hidden risks across websites and digital operations.

### Tone & Direction
- Not legal-heavy
- Not overly startup-like
- Calm, precise, structural
- Focus on “clarity” instead of “fear”

## 4. Section Details (English Copywriting)

### 4.1 Hero

**Headline**
Risks are most dangerous when they remain invisible.

**Sub Copy**
NIX™ reviews and structures hidden risks across your website and digital operations.  
From privacy flows to compliance gaps and operational inconsistencies, everything is analyzed in one unified framework.

---

### 4.2 Problem

**Title**
The absence of problems doesn’t mean the absence of risk.

**Copy**
Most companies assume that if their website is running, it is compliant.  
In reality, gaps accumulate silently across policies, user flows, content, and operational decisions.

**Key Points**
- Mismatch between policy and actual behavior  
- Fragmented ownership across teams  
- Industry-specific compliance gaps  
- Silent erosion of trust  

---

### 4.3 Perspective

**Title**
Risk doesn’t live in documents. It lives in flows.

**Copy**
Compliance is often treated as documentation.  
But real risk exists in how users interact with your product.

NIX™ focuses on:
- user flows  
- input structures  
- content expression  
- operational logic  

---

### 4.4 AI Workflow

**Title**
Faster, wider, and more structured review with AI

**Steps**

1. Collect  
We gather structure, content, flows, and policies  

2. Analyze  
We detect inconsistencies, missing elements, and risk patterns  

3. Structure  
We prioritize and translate findings into actionable insights
