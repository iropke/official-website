# iropke NOVA™ 페이지 정의 (기획 정리본)

---

## 1. Page Role

NOVA™ page must establish a new category:  
Branding is not just creation — it is continuous management.

User journey:

1. Awareness  
→ “Our brand feels inconsistent.”

2. Realization  
→ “Branding is breaking over time.”

3. Reframing  
→ “Brand is not a one-time output.”

4. Solution Understanding  
→ “We need a system, not guidelines.”

5. Action  
→ “We need ongoing brand control.”

---

## 2. Core Positioning

### One-line Definition
NOVA™ is an AI-powered brand architecture management solution that monitors and maintains brand consistency across all digital touchpoints.

### Core Concept
From Creation → To Continuity

### Tone & Direction
- Strategic and refined
- Calm authority
- Focus on consistency, control, and systemization
- Avoid “creative agency” tone, emphasize “structure + monitoring”

## 4. Section Details (English Copywriting)

### 4.1 Hero

**Headline**
Brand consistency doesn’t break instantly. It fades over time.

**Sub Copy**
NOVA™ ensures your brand stays consistent across content, design, and messaging.  
Using AI, it continuously monitors and detects elements that weaken your brand identity.

---

### 4.2 Problem

**Title**
Your brand is not broken. It’s drifting.

**Copy**
Most companies invest heavily in brand creation.  
But over time, inconsistency appears across channels, teams, and content.

This leads to:
- mixed messaging  
- visual inconsistency  
- diluted identity  

---

### 4.3 Perspective

**Title**
A brand is not a guideline. It’s a system.

**Copy**
Brand guidelines define intent.  
But real branding happens in execution.

Without continuous monitoring:
- teams interpret differently  
- content evolves inconsistently  
- identity weakens  

---

### 4.4 AI Monitoring

**Title**
Continuous brand monitoring with AI

**Copy**
NOVA™ scans and evaluates brand consistency across:

- messaging  
- tone  
- visual elements  
- content structure  

It identifies deviations before they accumulate.
